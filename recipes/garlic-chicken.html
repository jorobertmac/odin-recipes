Creamy Garlic Chicken with Mushrooms

Rich, flavorful, and perfect with pasta, rice, or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 8 oz cremini or button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried parsley (or fresh, for garnish)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken for 5-6 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  2. Lower the heat, add butter to the skillet, and sauté garlic for 1 minute. Add mushrooms and cook until softened (about 5 minutes).
  3. Stir in chicken broth, heavy cream, Parmesan, thyme, and parsley. Let simmer until the sauce thickens slightly (3-5 minutes).
  4. Return the chicken to the skillet, spoon the sauce over it, and let it simmer together for another 2 minutes.
  5. Serve with your favorite side—great with fettuccine or a side of crusty bread!
